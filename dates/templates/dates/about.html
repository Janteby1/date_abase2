About Date-abase

Date-abase is a collection of wonderful, exciting and romantic date ideas in and around the New York and New Jersy Area. The collection of dates was started as a small project between a group of friends who just needed some cool places to go.

Today, Date-abase is a thriving web site where users can search for date ideas by a variety of different criteria and can add their own ideas to the ever growing database. The Date-abase website started as a side project hobby and is continually being updated and upgraded with new and cool features so stay tuned to see what is in store!

Thank you and we hope you enjoy your date!

Best,
